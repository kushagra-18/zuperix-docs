# Core Concepts

Zuperix is more than just a folder on your computer. Here are the simple building blocks that make it so powerful.

## 📁 Workspaces

A **Workspace** is like a home for your projects. Everything in Zuperix belongs to a workspace. You can have one for your personal photography, another for your business, and a third for a specific client. 

*(Note: The ability to create multiple Workspaces is available exclusively on the **Gold Plan**. Bronze and Silver plans are limited to a single workspace.)*

- **Isolation**: Files in one workspace don't mix with others.
- **Collaboration**: Invite team members to specific workspaces.
- **Organization**: Each workspace has its own categories and collections.

## 🖼️ Assets

An **Asset** is any file you upload. It could be an image, a PDF, a 3D model, or even a video. In Zuperix, assets are "smart":

- **Metadata**: Each asset stores information like its color palette, size, and type automatically.
- **Versions**: If you upload a new version of the same file, Zuperix keeps the old ones so you never lose your progress.
- **Relations**: Assets can be grouped into categories or added to collections.

## 🗃️ Categories vs. Collections

Think of **Categories** like a permanent filing cabinet and **Collections** like a temporary project folder.

- **Categories**: Use these for hierarchical organization (e.g., `Marketing > 2024 > Summer Campaign`). An asset usually lives in one main branch of categories.
- **Collections**: These are flat groupings. An asset can be in as many collections as you want without moving its original location. Ideal for "Favorites" or "Pitch Deck Assets."
  - **Personal Collections**: Private to you. Perfect for grouping items you are currently working on without cluttering up the workspace for everyone else.
  - **Global Collections**: Shared with everyone in the workspace. Great for high-level groupings like "Final Approved Logos" or "Brand Playbook Assets."

## 🌐 Portals

A **Portal** is a public window into your workspace. It's a beautiful, customizable gallery that you can share with a simple link.

- **Sharing**: No more sending bulky email attachments. Just send a portal link.
- **Privacy**: Control who can see your portal and what they can download.
## 💠 Workspace vs. Portal

- **Workspace**: Your internal dashboard where you manage, organize, and edit assets.
- **Portal**: A public-facing gallery for sharing assets with clients or partners.

## 💎 Pricing Tiers & Feature Availability

Zuperix features are distributed across three main plans to grow with your team.

### 🥉 Bronze Plan ($10/mo)
*Simple and affordable for individuals.*
- **Users**: Up to 5
- **Storage**: 50GB Cloud Storage
- **Features**: Basic Search, Workflow Automation, Public Sharing Portals, OCR & Text Extraction.

### 🥈 Silver Plan ($25/mo)
*Great for small teams looking to stay organized.*
- **Users**: Up to 25
- **Storage**: 500GB Cloud Storage
- **Everything in Bronze, plus**:
    - **Smart AI Search**: Natural language prompts to find visual concepts.
    - **Facial Recognition**: Automatically group people in your photos.
    - **Public API & Webhooks**: Programmatic access to your assets.

### 🥇 Gold Plan ($50/mo)
*Powerful features for growing organizations.*
- **Users**: Unlimited
- **Storage**: 2TB Cloud Storage
- **Everything in Silver, plus**:
    - **Smart Tags**: AI automatically tags your assets upon upload.
    - **Advanced Search**: Relevance scoring and priority matching.
    - **Custom Branding**: Whitelabel your portals with your own logo and colors.
    - **SSO Authentication**: Secure sign-on for your entire team.
