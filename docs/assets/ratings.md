# Precision Curation with Asset Ratings ⭐

In a library of thousands, finding an asset is easy—but finding the **best** asset is where Zuperix shines. Our professional-grade rating system allows your team to collaboratively surface high-quality content and filter out the noise.

## The Power of Quality Control

Zuperix Asset Ratings transform your DAM from a simple storage vault into a curated library of excellence. 

### 🌟 Collaborative Feedback
Ratings aren't just for you; they are a shared language for your team.
- **5-Star Precision**: Rate assets from 1 to 5 stars to provide granular qualitative feedback.
- **Global Average**: Zuperix automatically calculates the **Global Average Rating** for every asset, giving you an instant snapshot of how the entire workspace values a particular file.

### 👤 Personalized Sentiment 
We understand that "quality" is sometimes subjective.
- **My Rating**: Every user has their own personal rating layer. Your 5-star rating won't overwrite your colleague's 3-star rating.
- **Contextual View**: When browsing the gallery, you'll see your own rating first, helping you find the assets *you* prefer while still seeing the community's score.

### 🔍 Advanced Quality Filtering
Ratings are integrated directly into our [Search Engine](../search/smart-search).
- **Top-Tier Discovery**: Use the filter sidebar or search shortcuts (like `rating:4+`) to instantly isolate your best work.
- **Automatic Curation**: Order your search results by "Top Rated" to ensure your highest-quality assets always appear first.

## Best Practices for Curation

- **Approval Workflows**: Use 5-star ratings to signify "Production Ready" assets and 1-star ratings for assets scheduled for archiving.
- **Smart Collections**: Create a [Global Collection](./organization#personal-vs-global) called "Best of the Best" and fill it with assets that have a 4.5+ average rating.
- **Auditioning Media**: When uploading a new marketing shoot, have your creative directors rate the candidates. The Global Average will quickly reveal the winners for the final campaign.
