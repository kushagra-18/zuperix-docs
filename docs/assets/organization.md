# Organizing Your Assets

Don't let your workspace become a mess. Zuperix gives you two powerful tools to organize your files: **Categories** and **Collections**.

## 📂 Categories (The Filing Cabinet)

Think of **Categories** as your permanent, hierarchical filing system. Use categories when an asset has one "true" home based on its nature or ownership.

### When to Use Categories
- **Strict Hierarchy**: `Marketing > 2024 > Summer Campaign`.
- **Departmental Folders**: `Legal`, `HR`, `Engineering`.
- **Global Organization**: These are shared across the entire workspace and provide the backbone of your library.

### 🏗️ Nested Structure
Zuperix supports unlimited nesting. Moving a category will automatically update the paths of all its thousands of children instantly.

---

## ⭐ Collections (The Project Playlist)

If categories are filing cabinets, **Collections** are playlists. An asset can live in one category but be part of ten different collections simultaneously without creating duplicates.

### When to Use Collections
- **Temporary Projects**: "Pitch Deck for Client X."
- **Personal Favorites**: Group your favorite stock photos for quick access.
- **Cross-Functional Groups**: "Approved Logos" (which might contain files from different brand categories).
- **Public Portals**: Collections are the primary way to power [Public Portals](../sharing/portals).

### 👥 Personal vs. Global
- **Personal Collections**: Private to you. Perfect for scratchpads and "Work in Progress" groupings.
- **Global Collections**: Shared with everyone in the workspace. Great for high-level groupings like "Final Approved Brand Assets."

---

## 🤖 Smart Labels & Metadata

Beyond your manual organization, Zuperix automatically layers "intelligence" onto every file.

- **Auto-Tags**: Our AI identifies over 1,000 distinct objects and concepts.
- **Custom Metadata**: Add business-critical fields like `Status`, `License Expiry`, or `Project Code`.
- **Search Everywhere**: Every category, collection, and tag is indexed in the [Smart Search bar](../search/smart-search).
