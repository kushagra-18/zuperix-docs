# Public Portals

Sharing assets has never been more beautiful. Zuperix lets you create public-facing galleries for your clients and team with ease.

## 📁 Creating Your First Portal

A **Portal** is a beautiful, public-facing window into your Zuperix workspace. Whether you're sharing a mood board with a client or a brand kit with your whole company, Portals make it look professional.

### 🍱 Curate Your Collection
You don't share your whole workspace; you share specific **Collections**.
1.  **Select a Collection**: In the sidebar, click the `...` next to any collection.
2.  **Enable Public Access**: Toggle the "Public Portal" switch.
3.  **Get Your Link**: Zuperix generates a unique URL (e.g., `portals.zuperix.com/p/your-unique-id`).

## 🏛️ Branded Identity (Gold Plan)

Zuperix isn't just about functionality; it's about making you look good. For **Gold Plan** customers, Portals become a seamless extension of your brand.

- **Custom Domain**: Remove `portals.zuperix.com` and use your own (e.g., `assets.yourcompany.com`).
- **Logo Overlays**: Remove the Zuperix logo and replace it with your own high-resolution brand mark.
- **Custom Color Palette**: Match the portal's primary colors to your brand guidelines.
- **Whitelabeling**: Remove all "Powered by Zuperix" mentions for a purely custom experience.

## 🔐 Privacy & Security

Sharing public links can be risky. Zuperix provides multiple layers of protection:

- **Password Protection (Silver+)**: Add a password requirement before anyone can view the portal.
- **Expiration Dates**: Set a "Self-Destruct" date for your links—perfect for temporary project pitches.
- **Download Controls**: Disable the "Download" button to allow users to view assets but not keep them.
- **Watermarking**: (Coming Soon!) Automatically overlay your logo on images to prevent unauthorized use.

## 📊 Analytics: Who's Looking?

Always know who's looking at your work.
- **Visit Count**: Track how many unique visitors have opened your portal.
- **Popular Assets**: See which files are getting the most attention and downloads.
- **Referrer Data**: Know if they found your portal from an email, Slack, or a direct link.
