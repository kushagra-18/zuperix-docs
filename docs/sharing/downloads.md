# High-Definition Downloads & Transformations 📥

Zuperix provides a sophisticated "On-the-Fly" transformation engine. Instead of storing ten different sizes of the same image, you can generate the exact version you need the moment you hit **Download**.

---

## 🍱 Instant Presets

For the most common workflows, we provide one-click presets that handle the resizing and optimization for you:

- **Small (800px)**: Optimized for mobile viewing and web-teasers. Uses **WebP** for maximum compression.
- **Large (2.4K)**: Designed for desktop hero images and presentations. High-fidelity **WebP**.
- **Transparent**: Converts the asset to a lossless **PNG**. Perfect for logos and assets with alpha channels.
- **Archival (HQ)**: The "Master" version. Downloads the original file in its native format (e.g., **TIFF** or High-Res JPG) without any quality loss.

---

## 🎨 Professional Customization

If the presets don't fit, use the **Customize** panel to build your own transformation:

- **Format Selection**: Convert instantly between **TIFF, PNG, JPG, and WebP**.
- **Aspect Ratio Locking**: Force the output into common ratios like `1:1 (Square)`, `16:9 (Widescreen)`, or `4:3`.
- **Output Dimensions**: Manually specify the exact Width (W) and Height (H) in pixels.
- **Image Quality**: A granular slider (1% to 100%) to balance file size against visual clarity.
- **Smart Cropping**: Use the **"Select Crop Option"** to manually define the focal point of your resized image.

---

## 📧 Asset Delivery (Email Direct)

No need to download a file just to upload it somewhere else. 

- **Direct Send**: Enter an email address directly in the download modal. Zuperix will process the transformation and send a secure, time-limited download link to the recipient.
- **Branded Experience**: The email arrives from your workspace, maintaining your brand's professional look.

---

## 📦 Batch & ZIP Processing

Downloading hundreds of items? Zuperix handles it in the background.

- **Bulk Packaging**: Select multiple assets and Zuperix will bundle them into a single, organized ZIP file.
- **Maintained Hierarchy**: Your custom category are preserved inside the ZIP.
- **Async Processing**: You can keep browsing the workspace while we prepare your high-volume download. We'll notify you when it's ready.
