# Canva Integration

The Zuperix Canva integration empowers your design teams by bridging the gap between your Digital Asset Management (DAM) environment and Canva's versatile creation suite. 

Access all your brand guidelines, licensed imagery, creative videos, and project folders without ever having to manage manual downloads or toggle separate browser tabs.

---

## Key Features

- **Centralized Asset Access**: Drag approved media directly onto your active Canva canvas effortlessly.
- **Search & Filters**: Narrow results by tags, workspaces, ratings, or orientations.
- **Bi-Directional Sync**: Export modified layouts straight into designated server categories safely.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Authenticate the App

1. Launch a blank or existing project inside the **Canva Editor**.
2. Navigate to the left-hand navigation panel and select **Apps**.
3. Type `Zuperix DAM` into the central search query box.
4. Click the app icon and tap the purple **Connect Account** button.
5. Provide your enterprise portal access keys inside the popup login prompt to map permissions.

### 2. Loading Library Media

Your complete corporate media array initializes natively under the custom drawer:
- **Search Operations**: Type any title to trigger dynamic matches instantly.
- **Dynamic Filters**: Open sidebar controls to restrict results accordingly.

---

## Uploading Completed Designs

If you wish to save brand assets directly:
1. Tap the standard **Share** trigger in the Canva top header.
2. Select the auxiliary `Zuperix` target format choice.
3. Determine target pools.
