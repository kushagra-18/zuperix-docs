# Upload Status Tracker

Uploading hundreds of files? Zuperix keeps you informed every step of the way.

## 🕒 Real-Time Progress

When you upload files, you don't have to wait for them to finish before continuing your work. Visit the **Upload Status** page in the sidebar to see:

- **Pending**: Files that are currently being processed.
- **Success**: Files that are now safe in Zuperix and ready to use.
- **Failed**: Any files that didn't go through, and what went wrong.

## 🤖 Smarter Processing

Behind the scenes, Zuperix isn't just saving your files; it's understanding them.

- **Wait for it**: Even after your file is "Uploaded," our AI might take a few seconds to extract tags, colors, and create previews.
- **Status Badges**: You'll see a small "Processing" badge on your assets in the main dashboard until the AI is finished.

## 📝 Tips for Successful Uploads

1. **Keep it steady**: For very large files, stay on the dashboard until the upload bar is full.
2. **Bulk is better**: You can select hundreds of images and upload them all at once.
3. **Format Support**: Upload images, PDFs, and 3D models with confidence.
