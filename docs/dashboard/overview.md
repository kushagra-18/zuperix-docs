# Dashboard Overview

Your central hub for all digital asset management. Accessible at [dashboard.zuperix.com](https://dashboard.zuperix.com), the Zuperix dashboard is designed to be intuitive and fast.

## 🗺️ The Great Map

Zuperix is designed to keep your focus on your assets, while keeping complex tools just one click away. Think of the dashboard as three distinct regions:

### 1. The Global Sidebar (Navigate)
Located on the far left, this is your primary compass.
- **🏠 Home**: Your personalized feed. Zuperix uses AI to suggest assets you were recently working on.
- **📁 Categories**: Your hierarchical library. Create and manage strict folder structures here.
- **⭐ Collections**: The "Playlists" of Zuperix. Group assets across different categories without moving the original files.
- **🆕 Upload Status**: A real-time monitor for large-scale batch uploads. 

### 2. The Search Frontier (Discover)
At the top of every page is the **Smart Search Bar**. It doesn't just search for text—it searches for **meaning**. 
- **AI Switch**: Toggle this ON to search by concepts (e.g., "a red car in the rain") instead of just filenames.
- **Dynamic Filters**: Based on your search, Zuperix will automatically suggest filters for colors, mime-types, and custom metadata fields.

### 3. The Asset Command Center (Manage)
The main grid isn't just a list of thumbnails; it's an interactive workspace.
- **Hover Actions**: Quickly download, share, or version an asset without opening it.
- **Details Panel**: Click any asset to open the right-side inspector. This is where you edit metadata, see AI-generated tags, and view version history.

## 🏷️ Bulk Actions

Need to move or delete many files at once? Simply select multiple assets and a toolbar will appear at the bottom, allowing you to:

- **Move to Collection**: Group many items quickly.
- **Delete**: Safely remove multiple files.
- **Share**: Create a portal for all selected items.
